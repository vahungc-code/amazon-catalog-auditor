What Your Audit Covers

Five checks. Zero guesswork.

Every check maps directly to Amazon's catalog rules. You get real findings about your listings, not generic advice.

Critical Listing suppressions
Missing Required Attributes

Fields Amazon requires to keep your listing active and buyable. If any are missing, your listing gets suppressed immediately.

Prohibited Characters

Restricted characters in your titles, bullets, and descriptions that trigger policy violations in your catalog.

Recommended Discoverability gaps
Missing Recommended Attributes

Optional attributes that have a direct impact on your search ranking, filter visibility, and conversion rate.

Title Policy Violations

Title length, formatting, and content rules that affect your policy compliance and click-through rate.

Insights Optimization opportunities
Bullets Content Quality

Completeness, length, and quality signals in your bullet points. Weak bullets cost you conversions and search placement.
